2.744 Product Design

DESCRIPTION AND PROCESS

Student teams of about six graduates and undergraduates from multiple disciplines collaborated with a customer each spring to produce new product ideas. Emphasis was on user experience, usability, product form, and marketability for the customer. During my semester, our client was Staples.

ROLE

Each student on the team brainstormed initial product ideas around the theme, 'eco-friendly office of the future,' helped refine product ideas, and helped maintain documentation of our work in our online portfolio. Lauren Gust and I were responsible for producing the final "looks-like" model.
