2.009 Product Engineering Process

DESCRIPTION AND PROCESS

My capstone design class produces alpha-level prototypes. The class is organized into teams of roughly 12 students each (primarily seniors in Mechanical Engineering). Inspired by the theme of the class, 'On the go,' Red Team produced a thermal regulating system for people with spinal cord injuries.

ROLE

My official responsibility on the team is to act as one of two System Integrators, coordinating design and manufacturing, running meetings, and tracking schedule. Two of my teammates and I were specifically responsible for technical design, manufacturing, and integration of the cooling system.
